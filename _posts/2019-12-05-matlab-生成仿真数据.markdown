### gen_bm_data.m
```
clear all;
close all;
clc;
fs = 250;
fs1= 20;
times = 1000;
y = gen_sin(fs, fs1, times)/10000;
for i=1:length(y)
    disp(sprintf('%f %f %f %f', y(i), y(i)/2, y(i)/4, y(i)/6));
end
```
